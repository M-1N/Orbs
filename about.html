What are O.R.B.S


O.R.B.S., or Obscure Radical Bounding Spheres, in which they have come to be known, is a phenomenon that has baffled researchers and enthusiasts alike. It refers to a series of unidentified objects that usually gather in groups of 3 that have been recorded around the world, appearing as blue spherical or orb-like entities that defy conventional scientific explanation. While, single instances of this phenomenon may seem peculiar, the sheer amount of these mysterious occurrences that have been captured on numerous panoramic images around the world prove otherwise.
